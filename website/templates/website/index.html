Kingdom Homes Stays was started 14 years ago by Eric Carmichael in Orlando, Florida. At first the company was only leasing single-family houses throughout the Central Florida area, however, shortly after our doors opened we realized there was a massive demand for single and multifamily short-term rentals as well. Our company most valuable asset is that we are property owners ourselves.

  • We practice what we preach.
  • We short term and lease our own properties.
  • We use the latest technology in the industry. It helps to provide greater transparency and reduced costs to ourselves and our clients - A win-win.

Our customer service is what separates us from our competition because we live it and have proven methods that we use, and we offer it to other owners who want the benefit but don't have the time or experience to maximize their property's true value in an expedited time frame. We carefully select our clients to make sure their core values align with those of Kingdom Homes Stays.

Why Choose Kingdom Homes?
